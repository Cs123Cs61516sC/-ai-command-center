1.4 Gatekeeper Quick Loops

  • Help‑Me Frame – politely enlist assistance, keeps control.
  • Voicemail Offer – “Should I just leave a 15‑sec voicemail for {{Name}} so they can decide?”
  • Role Clarifier – if unsure, ask who owns the specific KPI the AI improves.

2 | 5‑Minute Pitch Outline (Phase 0 Call)

  1. Warmth (30 sec) – quick connection question (NEPQ stage 1).
  2. Problem Surfacing (1 min) – 2‑3 situational questions to quantify pain.
  3. Solution Teaser (1 min) – share one relevant micro‑case with ROI %.
  4. Tax & Write‑Off Hook (45 sec) – highlight §174 amortization & OPEX deductions.
  5. Engagement Close (1 min) – “Would it make sense to map this in a short workshop?”
