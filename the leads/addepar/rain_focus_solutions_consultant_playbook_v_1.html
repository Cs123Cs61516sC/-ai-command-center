RainFocus Solutions Consultant – Executive Brief (v4)

Why Now?

Event‑tech clients expect rapid onboarding and flawless CRM/data integrations. RainFocus’ Solutions Consultants are pivotal—but expensive. AI automation can cut cycle time, boost client satisfaction, and save >65 % compared with hiring another SC.

Project Scope

Phase 1 builds an AI‑driven configuration engine; Phase 2 layers data validation & CRM sync; Phase 3 institutionalises dashboards and human‑override controls.

Solution Overview

  1. Event‑template intake wizard.
  2. LLM‑generated configuration checklist.
  3. Schema normalization + validation.
  4. Real‑time integration monitor & exception routing.
  5. Metrics dashboard & Slack/MS Teams alerts.

Projected ROI

Breakeven in ~6 months; Year‑2+ savings ≈ $100 k annually; implementation time cut 50‑60 %.
