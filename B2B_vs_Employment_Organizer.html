Hybrid Strategy Recommendations

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

  • Focus on 1-2 quick B2B wins ($10-50K projects)
  • Apply to high-value W2 positions as backup
  • Accept short-term 1099 contracts for cash flow

Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-9)

  • Scale B2B to 3-5 active clients
  • Consider part-time W2 for stability
  • Build recurring revenue streams

Phase 3: Scale (Months 10+)

  • B2B should be 70%+ of revenue
  • Selective 1099 for strategic clients
  • W2 only if equity/leadership role
