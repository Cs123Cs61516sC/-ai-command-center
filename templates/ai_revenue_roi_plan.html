AI Opportunity Playbook & Vetting Template

Reusable checklist to assess any midsize manufacturer or service outfit. All numeric examples use a hypothetical $10 M‑revenue company; swap with client‑specific data.

0 | Methodology – Repeatable Eight‑Step Path

  1. Rapid Public‑Data Sweep – Pull D&B, Buzzfile, LinkedIn head‑count, BBB, website crawl. Establish revenue band & digital maturity.
  2. Multi‑Pass Deep Dive – Seven thematic passes (Reg/Safety, Digital Footprint, Channel Reach, Material Shift, Tech/IP, Governance, FinOps).
  3. Pain‑Point Validation – Split findings into Known (stated by prospect) vs. Hidden (inferred gaps).
  4. Lead‑Type Mapping – Identify every revenue persona (specifier, contractor, distributor, etc.).
  5. Solution Catalogue – Brainstorm automation levers; score each for Impact ($), Effort (dev weeks), Cultural Fit.
  6. Financial Modeling – Overlay Lean vs. Full scenarios on baseline P&L; sensitivity on close‑rate, margin swing, payroll release.
  7. Integration Audit – Quick poll of accounting, ERP, CRM, TMS, IoT stack. Tag connectors as “shim now” or “deep API later.”
  8. Decision Gates & Road‑map – 90‑day pilot, ROI checkpoint, Year‑1 expansion go/no‑go, Year‑2 deep tech gate.

Ask every prospect for: recent P&L export, estimator timesheets, ad‑spend breakdown, production logs.

4 | Decision Checklist

  1. Confirm revenue band via multiple public sources.
  2. Crawl website: capture forms, download any quote/order docs.
  3. Populate Pain‑Point Matrix.
  4. Select Lean vs. Full solution set.
  5. Complete Financial Template with client data.
  6. Present build cost vs. ROI; gate on 90‑day pilot.

5 | Evidence Trail (swap for client specifics)

  • D&B or Buzzfile revenue range.
  • Website artefacts (order forms, missing analytics tags).
  • API vendor pricing pages (imagery, LLM tokens).
  • Any relevant compliance or regulatory docs.
