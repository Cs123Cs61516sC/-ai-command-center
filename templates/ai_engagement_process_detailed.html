6. Change‑Management & Exit Clauses

  • Scope creep: Any feature not in the roadmap triggers a written change request priced at $600/hr.
  • Cancellation: Either party may terminate with 14 days notice; client pays for accepted work + in‑progress hours at published rates.
  • IP Ownership: Client owns deliverables; supplier retains reusable models & libraries licensed royalty‑free to client.
  • SLA Targets post‑launch: 99.5 % uptime, <150 ms latency p95, model drift check every 30 days.
