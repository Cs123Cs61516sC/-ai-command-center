AI Consulting Opportunity Playbook (Reusable Master Template)

Purpose: Single, drop‑in framework to vet prospects, craft ROI‑driven road‑maps, price work and generate a polished Executive Brief for decision‑makers — all in one file.

0 | Quick‑Start Workflow

  1. Job‑Posting Harvest – Clip target roles/projects (Indeed, LinkedIn, Upwork, etc.). Note pains and keywords.
  2. Background & Vetting Pass – Use Section 1 tables & Brody/Kevin/Karen/Pinko lenses.
  3. Eight‑Step Opportunity Scan – Fill Section 2 matrix to surface high‑ROI automations.
  4. ROI Ladder + Cost Calc – Complete Section 3 tables to quantify Lean vs. Full build.
  5. Engagement Timeline & Pricing – Select consult tier ($7 K solo / $10 K w/ BD) & outline 45‑day MVP.
  6. Draft Executive Brief – Copy Section 5 template, populate {{placeholders}}.
  7. Send Pack to Management – Management emails brief + pricing, opens dialogue.
