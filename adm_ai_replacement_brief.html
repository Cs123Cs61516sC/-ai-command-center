RainFocus Solutions Consultant – AI Consulting Opportunity Playbook (v16.1)

Prepared for RainFocus • 2 Jul 2025

1. Executive Summary

RainFocus uses costly human Solutions Consultants (≈ $140 k fully‑loaded each) to configure event workflows and integrate CRM/data pipelines. Peak‑season backlogs delay client go‑live by up to four weeks. An AI‑driven intake, mapping, and validation stack can eliminate 65 % of consultant labour, cut onboarding cycles 50‑60 %, and accelerate revenue recognition—all while preserving a small human tier for edge‑case handling.

2. Current State & Pain Points

  • Manual schema mapping and data validation dominate consultant hours.
  • Onboarding backlog (3–4 wks) hurts NPS and delays billed usage.
  • CRM sync errors trigger SLA penalties and re‑work costs.

3. Solution Overview

The proposed AI system ingests event specs, auto‑generates configuration checklists, maps data schemas, performs live validation, and pushes clean records to Salesforce/HubSpot/Adobe. A monitoring layer surfaces exceptions to a lean human team, with KPIs fed to a leadership dashboard.
